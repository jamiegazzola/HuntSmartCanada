How it works

From zero to optimized in four steps.

01
Choose your province

Select BC or Alberta. Each province has its own draw system — HuntSmart covers both, with BC data going back 25+ years and Alberta data spanning 3+ seasons.

02
Set your profile (Alberta)

Enter your residency status and priority points once per species category. HuntSmart uses your exact point total to calculate your personal draw odds across every Alberta draw.

03
Filter by map, species & odds

Click WMUs directly on the interactive map to pick your region visually. Then filter by species, minimum draw odds, and harvest success rate to find draws worth applying for.

04
Dig into your draws

Every draw card shows annual odds going back up to 25 years, 10-year harvest success rates, and — for Alberta — your personal draw odds at your exact priority level.

What's inside

Everything you need to apply smarter.

📊
25+ Years of BC Draw History

Every LEH draw code in British Columbia tracked year by year — applications, permits, and odds. See exactly how competitive a draw has been over time, not just last season.

🎯
Personal Alberta Odds

Enter your priority points once. HuntSmart calculates your exact draw probability for every Alberta draw — resident and non-resident breakdowns included.

🗺️
Interactive WMU Map

Click any Wildlife Management Unit across BC or Alberta to instantly see its draws, odds, and species data.

🏆
Harvest Success Rates

Draw odds only tell half the story. See 10-year harvest success rates so you know whether a permit actually translates to a kill.

🔍
Smart Filtering

Filter simultaneously by species, WMU, minimum draw odds, and minimum harvest success. Results update live as you adjust.

🔖
Save & Compare

Bookmark draws you're considering and pull up a side-by-side comparison to weigh your options before application deadlines hit.
